# EUROGATE Context for Challenge Framing

This note summarizes public context relevant to why reefer demand forecasting matters.

## Company and Terminal Network

EUROGATE is a container terminal operator with a network that includes locations such as:

- Bremerhaven
- Hamburg
- Wilhelmshaven
- Tangier
- Limassol
- and Italian terminals such as La Spezia, Ravenna, and Salerno (via network interests)

Primary reference:

- [EUROGATE terminals page](https://www1.eurogate.de/en/terminals/)

## Why This Forecasting Problem Is Business-Critical

Reefer loads are operationally important because they:

- vary with weather and cargo temperature needs
- contribute to power peaks at terminal level
- can impact infrastructure planning and operating cost

For terminal modernization and electrification programs, better short-horizon load forecasts support:

- charging and equipment scheduling
- peak-risk mitigation
- power contract and capacity planning

## Practical Interpretation for This Hackathon

- Challenge scope: forecast next-day hourly reefer demand and p90 risk estimate.
- Product direction: build a modular forecasting stack that can later absorb richer exogenous signals for planning use cases.
